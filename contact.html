Quick Support Guide

🐛 Found a Bug?

Please describe what you were doing when the issue occurred, what happened, and what you expected to happen. Include your browser information and device type. Send to our bug report email.

🚀 Feature Request?

We'd love to hear your ideas! Tell us what feature you'd like to see and how it would improve your experience. Our development team reviews all suggestions.

⚕️ Medical Question?

For medical advice or concerns, please consult with a licensed healthcare professional. Our support team can only provide technical assistance about using the app, not medical guidance.

🔐 Privacy Concerns?

If you have questions about your data or privacy, email us at privacy@healthcareassistant.com. We take your privacy seriously and comply with all regulations.
