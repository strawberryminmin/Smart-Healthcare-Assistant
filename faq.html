Frequently Asked Questions

📱 How do I use the voice input feature? +

Click the microphone button next to the symptom input field. Grant permission to access your microphone when prompted. Speak clearly about your symptoms, and the system will transcribe your words. Once done, click the mic button again to stop recording.

🌍 What languages are supported? +

Our assistant supports over 100 languages including Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic, Japanese, Portuguese, and many more. You can select your preferred language before generating the report.

🔒 Is my personal health information safe? +

Yes, your health information is treated with strict confidentiality. We follow HIPAA guidelines and industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data. Your information is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

🩹 What should I do if the assistant says it's an emergency? +

If the system identifies an emergency situation (chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, etc.), DO NOT rely solely on this assistant. Call your local emergency number (911 in the US) or go to the nearest hospital immediately. This assistant can only provide basic guidance and is not a substitute for emergency medical care.

💊 Can I use this instead of visiting a doctor? +

No, this assistant is designed to provide basic first-aid guidance and initial symptom analysis only. It does NOT replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for persistent symptoms, medication prescriptions, or chronic conditions.

📊 What vital signs should I record? +

If possible, record:

  • Temperature: In Fahrenheit or Celsius (use a thermometer)
  • Blood Pressure: Format (systolic/diastolic) like 120/80 mmHg
  • Pulse Rate: Number of beats per minute (feel your pulse or use a pulse oximeter)

If you don't have equipment, leave these fields as estimated values.

🎤 Will the system understand my accent or speech patterns? +

Our voice recognition system is trained to understand various accents and speech patterns. However, for best results, speak clearly and avoid background noise. If the system misunderstands, you can always type your symptoms instead.

📄 Can I print or save my health report? +

Yes! After generating a report, you can use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to save it as a PDF or print it. You can also download the report and share it with your doctor for reference.

✅ What if I don't know exact measurements for vital signs? +

You can enter estimated values or use common reference points. For example, normal temperature is around 98.6°F (37°C), normal blood pressure is around 120/80 mmHg, and normal pulse is 60-100 beats per minute. The system will account for variations.

🔄 How often can I use the assistant? +

You can use the assistant as often as needed. There are no usage limits. However, if your symptoms persist or worsen, please seek professional medical help instead of relying continuously on this system.
